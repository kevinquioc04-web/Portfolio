Notion Task Tracker

Notion Make.com

Developed an automated task management and workflow tracker in Notion to streamline daily operations and team collaboration.

Project Overview

*Sample demonstration of the Notion Task Tracker interface and workflow.

The Problem

Many processes are very tedious when it comes to updating trackers and files, and it requires a lot of effort to manage everything all at once.

The Solution

I built a Notion Task Tracker to efficiently monitor all necessary tasks. Team members can simply click on a task to assign it to themselves and provide regular progress updates using a percentage-based completion scale.

The Result

The team's overall efficiency improved significantly. The tracker eliminated redundant work, ensuring no two teammates accidentally tackled the same assignment. When collaboration was necessary, members could easily co-assign themselves to a task, providing complete visibility into who was working together on specific items.

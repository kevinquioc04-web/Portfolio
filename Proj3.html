CRM Workflow Optimization

Salesforce • Zendesk • Make.com

The Problem

Data silos between customer support (Zendesk) and sales (Salesforce) led to a 20% error rate in customer records and frustrated non-technical stakeholders.

The Solution

I used Make.com to build a low-code automation that syncs ticket metadata into Salesforce accounts automatically. I also created custom views in Salesforce tailored for non-technical users.

The Result

Increased data accuracy by 30% and reduced the "time-to-insight" for the sales team, as they no longer had to request manual reports from the data team.

Startup Inventory & Sales Database

Excel • SQL • Notion

The Problem

A growing startup was tracking sales on paper and disconnected spreadsheets, causing frequent stock-outs and unknown profit margins.

The Solution

I built a relational database system using SQL logic within Excel to track inventory movement against daily sales. I then integrated a Notion front-end for the warehouse team to update stock levels easily via mobile.

The Result

Achieved 100% inventory accuracy and provided the founder with a YTD growth dashboard that helped secure their next round of micro-funding.
